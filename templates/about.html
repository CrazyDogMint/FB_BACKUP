About

This tool is made by Kong in Taiwan. But the site you see may be maintained by other people.

You can download data from Facebook, including comments of each post and photo, without losing data of author, time, tags and links. You can consider the exported data as a backup of Facebook. Only your own data is possible to download.

This site is a standalone version of the project and established on GitHub Pages. There's no server-side language here (except Facebook SDK, for which Facebook is responsible), and thus your data is not saved by the server.
